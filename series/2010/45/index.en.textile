With more than 800 artworks of the russian avant-garde the Museum Ludwig has one of the most extensive collections. Last year an exhibition dealed with the beginning of modern art in Russia ("Eine Ohrfeige dem öffentlichen Geschmack." Der Kubofuturismus und der Aufbruch der Moderne in Russland). The exhibition this year is concentrating on Kasimir Malewitsch and is showing the whole collection of his work.

*Kasimir Malewitsch und der Suprematismus in der Sammlung, Museum Ludwig Köln, bis 20. Feb. 2011*
