The Ikarus-myth, dealing with the relation between utopia and reality, might be symptomatical for the DDR and it has indeed often been interpreted by artists of the DDR.
Among the 260 artworks of the DDR, which are presented in the exhibition "Abschied von Ikarus" in Weimar, there is not only state art, but the art of subcultures and intermediate positions as well. The exhibition efforts to give an objective and differentiated view on DDR-art.
