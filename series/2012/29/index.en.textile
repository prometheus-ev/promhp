Monumental paintings and sculptures by Anselm Kiefer are shown in the broad rooms of the Bundeskunsthalle in Bonn.
The exhibition is presenting examples of Kiefers artwork with their characteristic diversity of material and some of the main topics. Kiefer deals with past and time, as well as with thinking. The announcement of the museum quotes Kiefer, who said, that he ‘pokes a hole, and then goes through it’ and explains it as a searching for the story behind the story.
