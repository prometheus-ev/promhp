In the year 1937 Solomon R. Guggenheim created in New York a foundation, which was the basis for the now famous collection of the so called Gugggenheim-Museum. Donations and aquisitions expended the inventory. Today there are significant exhibits from Impressionism and Postimpressionism as well as Expressionism and Surrealism. A current exhibition in Berlin includes furthermore art from the 21st century and transforms the review into a plea for the support of temporary art.
