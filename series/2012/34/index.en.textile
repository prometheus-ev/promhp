The author and painter Francis Picabia is described as a contrarian, who does not fit into any pigeonhole. His entire oevre is characterized by many changes of style in a radical way. A retrospective in the Kunsthalle Krems provides the opportunity to examine the work by Picabia in relation to modernism and postmodernism.
