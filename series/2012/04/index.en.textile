When at the beginning of the 20th century architects didn't found adequate furniture for their plans, they created it by themselves. But not later than in the middle of the century the job profile of an architect was separated from that of a designer. Nevertheless there are still architects, who create in specific cases furniture for their architecture.
The MAK in Köln raises the questions, if the furniture-design made by architects differs from that made by designers and if there is a typical kind of creating furniture, which is shared by all architects in all times.
