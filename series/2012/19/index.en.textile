Hundred years ago the almanac „Der blaue Reiter“ was published. Resigned from the „Neue Künstlervereinigung München“ Wassily Kandinsky and Franz Marc worked together to show their ideas to modern art, music and stage design in a publication. After getting the voucher copies at the 11th May 1912 Marc wrote to Kandinsky, that the impression of the book is a fabulous one and that  many young forces will get enthusiastic about it.
