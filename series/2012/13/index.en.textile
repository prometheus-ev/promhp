In partnership with the Tate Modern and the Centre Pompidou emerged an exhibition in the National Gallery in Berlin on the occasion of the 80th Birthday of Gerhard Richter.  
To focus on the variety of his work in every period, they decided to arrange it chronological: figurative works are hanging next to abstract ones, grey ones next to colourful ones. Beginning and ending with new art works, the exhibition is presenting the panorama of a lifework.
