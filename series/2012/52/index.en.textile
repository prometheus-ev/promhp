Images of the studio can be revealing about the artist and his self-perception. An exhibition at the Staatsgalerie Stuttgart is presenting pictures of studios from the 19th to the 21st century, showing different forms of self-representation. "The poor poet" by Spitzweg deals with the clich√© of the poor artist: Nothing can stop his creative power, even in adverse conditions. The studiopainting by Beckmann seems to based on the idea of creative chaos. While Liebermanns work shows a representative Salon with correctly dressed women, Kirchner and Matisse were painting their nude models in cosy atmosphere.
