Many other self-portraits followed the early one, which Dürer draw in the age of 13 or 14. A current exhibition deals, next to other four themes, with the figurative and the written credentials of self-expression of this artist from Nuremberg. The exhibition is analysing Dürers self-representation in relation to the self-representation of Nuremberg`s middle class in the 15th century.
