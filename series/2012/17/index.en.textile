An exhibition of the foundation Moritzburg in Halle analyses the alteration of portraits of academics in the history. There is a changing from a conventional scheme to very private portraits. In our examples obvious attributes are glasses, paper and pencil, which demonstrate the shown people as scientists and thinkers. 300 memberportraits of the oldest natural scientific-medical academy, the Leopoldina, are presented in the Moritzburg to show, if the portraits keep these attributes over the centuries.
