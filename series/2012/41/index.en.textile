With his minimalist „black paintings“ Frank Stella was an important representative of  the American art of the 1960ies. Since then Stella's works developed from minimalism to a „maximalism“, they became more colourful and more and more three-dimensional. The Museum of Art in Wolfsburg is presenting a retrospective with 60 large-sized paintings and sculptures, as well as 80 paper works. The hanging is made by Stella himself and is not chronological, but is correlating works from different times.
