The varied work of Karl Friedrich Schinkel includes architecture, paintings and graphic art, as well as interior design and stage design. An exhibition of the Kupferstichkabinett Berlin is part and result of a great project concerning the research, preservation and mediation of Schinkels artistic legacy. The exhibition asks about his importance for the history of architecture, as well as for the development of decorative arts. Another focus is on his view of history, witch is for example expressed in patriotic motifs.
