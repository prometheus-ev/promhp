As a place for queer fellows and outsiders, as a parallel existence to the middle-class society, the circus was a famous topic in art at many times, especially in the modernism. A current exhibition in Vienna is presenting artistic statements concerning the topic circus by contemporary artist.

*"The circus as a Parallel Universe":http://www.kunsthallewien.at/cgi-bin/event/event.pl?id=4597&lang=en, May the 4th to September the 2nd 2012, Kunsthalle Wien, hall 1*
