An exhibition in Bayreuth is presenting art works by Lyonel and Andreas Feininger. Correlating the cityscapes of the father, showing small German towns and villages, and the one of the son, with  pictures of the metropolis New York, there are astonishing many parallels in the composition of the images. The art works of both artists give an idea of the fascination that emenates from urban structures.
