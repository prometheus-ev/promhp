Frederick the Great typified the so called „Prussian virtues“ of hard work, order and sense of duty. Also he will remain in the cultural memory as a philosopher, a proponent of the Enlightenment and a reformer. Negative connotations call him a warmonger and a humans-spurner. The “Stiftung Preußische Schlösser und Gärten Berlin-Brandenburg” sees his 300th anniversary as a chance to present this character in different facets.
