Two exhibitions in Stuttgart, subsumed under the title "The world of the Celts", are presenting Celtic objects from all over Europe. The main focus in the exhibition "Centres of power" is on early Celtic residences and late Celtic towns and is dealing with economic, social and religious aspects of the culture. The complex ornamentation, among other things in the book illumination of the Celts, is the main interest of the second exhibition: "Treasures of art".
