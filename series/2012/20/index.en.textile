There are different types of pictures to illustrate the ascension of Christ, derived from the description by the evangelist Luke. An ivory relief from around 400 is showing Christ, who ascends a mountain where the hand of God receives him. An other type of pictures is showing two areas: on earth the disciples are standing with maria and are looking up to Christ, who hovers above them surrounded by angels, or whose feet just disappear in the clouds. The type of a free ascending Christ is detectable since the 13th century.
