The art works by Rosemarie Trockel (1952) and Paloma Varga Weisz (1966) show sometimes similar topics, for example a kind of self-reflection as a female artist in a male dominated world of art. An exhibition by Trockel and Varga Weisz in Schloss Morsbroich will be influenced by the interaction with each other and the examination of the atmosphere of the castle.
