With three significant groups of work the Fondation Beyeler is showing the art by Jeff Koons in Basel. Including on one hand the brand new household appliances presented in plexiglass cases, on the other hand the kitchy sculptures made of porcelain or wood and at last the monumental steel-sculptures. The Fondation Beyeler is presenting the first exhibition with art works by Jeff Koon in a Swiss museum.
