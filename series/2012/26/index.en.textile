Karl Hagemeister
Landscapes 

The landscape paintings by Karl Hagemeister are rooted in his homeland, the Mark Brandenburg. An exhibition in the Börhan-Museum is characterising Hagemeister as an artist, who lived reclusive to pay hommage to the nature. On the other hand Hagemeister lived not at all withdrawn: He was interested in the development of contemporary art, did study trips to the Netherlands, to Italy and France, and was one of the first members of the "Berliner Secession".
