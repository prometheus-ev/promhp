On the occasion of the 100th birthday of Louise Bourgeois, who deceased in 2010, the Hamburger Kunsthalle is presenting art works from the last 15 years of her life. Often her works are provocative, strange, sometimes even frightening and at the same time they bristle with energy, directness and vitality.
Especially prominent is the oversized bronze-spider „Maman“ of over 9 meters, which is regarded as a key work. The web spinning insect became a homage to the mother of the artist, who worked as a restorer of tapestries.
