The people from Vienna had problems with the three naked men on a poster for the present exhibition in the Leopold Museum. The picture by the French artists Pierre & Gilles have caused a public scandal. Therefore the relevant parts needed to be covered with a red bar. Is it still a taboo to display naked men? Not one but two Austrian exhibitions are now dealing with the male nude in art of the 19th to the 21st century.
