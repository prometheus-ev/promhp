The authority of da Vinci and of the church seem to inspire parodies and provocations. There is a wide range from an artistic enthronement over a political protest to an advertising strategy. Andy Warhol varied the prototype in coloured serigraph; Ben Willikens deleted the figures from the composition and Mary Beth-Edelson replaced them with portraits of female American artists; Otto Kern and Brigitte Niedermaier got attention for fashion-labels by showing models scantily clad and Rene√© Cox showed her protest against the catholic church with a nude and female Christ.
