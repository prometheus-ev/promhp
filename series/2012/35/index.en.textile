An exhibition in the Martin-Gropius-Bau in Berlin is dealing with the ancient origin of the Olympic Games. Organised since 1894 the competitions have an ancient tradition: They are generated as a part of the Zeus-cult in the sanctuary of Olympia. The exhibition is presenting a description of the god cult, will explain the ancient sport-types and is showing a detailed report of the excavations in Olympia.
