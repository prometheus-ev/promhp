The girls with flowers are one example of motifs, which Karl Hofer (1878 – 1955) was revising over decades. Among them, there are the boys with a ball, female nudes, people reading and writing or playing an instrument, circus-pictures and parties. An exhibition in the Kunsthalle Emden is concentrated on these in motifs and is presenting art works from five decades with a focal point on the 1920th and 1930th.
 
*"Karl Hofer. Von Lebensspuk und stiller Schönheit":http://kunsthalle-emden.de/ab-11-02-karl-hofer-von-lebensspuk-und-stiller-schonheit/, bis 17. Juni 2012, Kunsthalle Emden*
