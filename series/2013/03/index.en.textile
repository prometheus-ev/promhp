In paintings, drawings and prints, on glass and on ceramics Marc Chagall processes scenes from the Bible. The fantastical pictures by the painter, who had grown up in Russia in a Jewish family, connect his personal wealth of experiences with traditional Christian images. Chagalls design for glass windows for big churches in France, England and Germany, got famous.

This week ended an exhibition, which gave a broad view on that topic with about 140 exhibits.
