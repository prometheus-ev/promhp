Zeus, Athena, Hermes and Dionysos ...the world of Greek gods is handed down in sculptures and vase paintings. An exhibition at the Glyptothek in Munich is conveying the ancient Greek mythology. The different deities and some of their stories around love and dispute are presented. Furthermore is shown, how present the gods were in daily life and how they were adored in Greek sanctuaries.
