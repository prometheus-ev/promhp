An exhibition in the August-Macke-Haus in Bonn is dedicated to the art works by Heinrich Maria Davringhausen (1894 - 1970). They are presenting not only his figurative paintings of the 1920s, but also some of the few preserved early works and such of the surrealist and abstract late work, which is not widely known in Germany.
