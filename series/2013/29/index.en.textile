A Nabis-exhibition in the Neue Pinakothek in Munich is mainly an occasion to present the own collection. For the first time all pictures of the Nabis represented in the stock are shown together. Art works of Vallotton, Bonnard, Maillol, Vuillard and Denis are part of the show as well as works of their pioneers like Gauguin, van Gogh and Puvis de Chavannes. The exhibition´s title  »Paris intense« refers to a graphic-series made by Félix Vallotton in 1893/94, shortly after he joined the artist group Nabis.

*"Paris intense. The Nabis - From Bonnard to Vallotton":http://www.pinakothek.de/en/nabis, 04 July to 30 September 2013, Neue Pinakothek, Munich
