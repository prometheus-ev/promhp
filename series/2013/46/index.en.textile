On the occasion of his 100 birthday the Schirn Kunsthalle is presenting the late work of Philip Guston. Beeing a representative of abstract Expressionism before, Guston had turned to figurative painting in the years around 1970. After the Hamburger Kunsthalle had an exhibition with the work of Ronald B. Kitaj, the Schirn is showing the work of another artist, who did not follow the primacy of abstract art in the American painting of the postwar period.

*"Philip Guston. Late works":http://www.schirn.de/en/Page2919.html, 6 November 2013 to 2 February 2014, Schirn Kunsthalle, Frankfurt am Main*
