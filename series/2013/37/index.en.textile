On the occasion of the 100th birthday of Meret Oppenheim there is a retrospective in her native town Berlin. In the early 30s she acted as a model to Man Rays photographies „Érotique voilée“, but the surrealists respected her as an artist too. The exhibition in the Martin-Gropius-Bau  underlines Oppenheims emancipated attitude and the humorous side of her work. Exhibited are works of her entire œuvre.
