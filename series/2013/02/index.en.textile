An exhibition in the "Kunsthalle" in Kiel recalled the anniversary of the death of Christian Rohlfs, who died 75 years ago in 1938. Having a big amount of art works by Rohlfs in their stock, the Kunsthalle is able to exhibit a comprehensive overview. The exhibition includes all creative periods of the artist, starting with the impressionistic works from before and around 1900 and ending with the expressionistic paintings from in the last 20 years of his life. Many of the shown pieces are exhibited for the very first time.
