With 37 places Germany is one of the countries with the most UNESCO World Heritage sites. Up to now the main interest was on the effect of the World Heritage status on tourism. Now the museum for architecture in Frankfurt am Main is asking which influence the World Heritage status of a building or an ensemble of buildings has on architectural and urban planning of the environment.
