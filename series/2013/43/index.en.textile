The exhibition in Düsseldorf with photographies by Candida Höfer is showing solely artworks being made in Düsseldorf. The city, were Höfer attended the art college and were she had her first solo exhibition, marks the beginning of her artistic work. The Museum Kunstpalast is presenting photographs of interiors and architecture as well as pictures from her first series „Turks in Germany“.
