An exhibition in Nürnberg shows how current the principles of Minimal Art still are. Reduced forms and colours, serial repetition and industrial production are design tendencies, which are important for many generations of artists. The exhibition is presenting manly unknown works of the Goetz-Collection. The circa 30 artists either formed the minimalism, were affected by it or found their own way of minimalistic art.
