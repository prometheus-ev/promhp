The Iroquois had caused much fascination in Europe. An exhibition in Bonn is demonstrating, that they became protagonists of literature and visual arts as fearsome warriors and skilled diplomats. 
The cooperation of the Bundeskunsthalle with Iroquoian artists, curators and intellectuals ensures, that the topic is not limited to the European view on Iroquoian culture. With ethnographic objects and Iroquoian contemporary art the development of their culture is presented up to the 21st century.
