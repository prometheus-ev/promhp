The cultural heritage of Florence is subject of an exhibition at the Bundeskunsthalle. Paintings, sculptures, drawings, textiles and records illustrate the history of that city in the toscana region. The exhibits not only convey a cultural development, but are a testimony for the scientific, economic, politic and religious change of Florence from the 15th to the 19th century, too.
