Gerhard Altenbourg, born in 1926 as Gerhard Ströch, is subject of a retrospective in Durach. Living in the DDR Altenbourg practiced his art with a distinctive individualism. In 1959 he was represented at the second documenta at Kassel.

The retrospective in Durbach had arosen in cooperation with the Lindenau-Museum Altenburg and is presenting a selection of 150 art works from all phases of work between 1948 and 1989.
