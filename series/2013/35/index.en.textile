An exhibition in Gottdorf deals with realism in Northern Germany painting. The museum is presenting works of an artist group, who organised symposia since 20 years to do outdoor painting. The exhibition shows  these pictures as well as some from the studio.

*"Realismus in Noddeutschland. Eine Zwischenbilanz":http://www.realismus-in-norddeutschland.de/, 26 May to 20 October 2013, Schloss Gottdorf, Schleswig*
