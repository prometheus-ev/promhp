On the occasion of the 100th anniversary of Karl Otto Götz the Neue Nationalgalerie is holding a retrospective of his work. As a representative of Informal art, Götz invented a special painting-technique: He applies the paint on a canvas, witch is covered with paste, and continues his work with a brush and a squeegee. The exhibition shall focus on the serial processes in the development of his visual ideas.
