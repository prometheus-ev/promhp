The work by Alberto Giacometti is currently presented in two exhibitions in Hamburg. The one in the Bucerius Kunst Forum is dedicated to the portraits, while the one in the Kunsthalle is dealing with the room concepts in the work of Giacometti. Famous are especially the sculptures, where several of his typical figures in overlength are arranged on a plinth â€“ like on a playing field. This exhibition intends furthermore to emphasize the importance of the early surrealistic work for the room concepts by Giacometti.
