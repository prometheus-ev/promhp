Nancy Graves has exhibited her work in Europe for the first time 1971 in Aachen. Now a Museum in Aachen will present a retrospective of her work, concentrated on art works from the 1970s. Next to around 70 sculptures, installations, drawings, paintings and films by Graves, they will show works of other temporary artists, to allow a revaluation of the art of Nancy Graves.
