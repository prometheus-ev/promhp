Nowadays the analysis of paintings using scientific methods is popular. The Wallraf-Richartz-Museum in Cologne is presenting the results of a perennial research project. Medieval paintings of their collection have been analysed to get more informations about the way they were made and used, about the age determination and the artist. The exhibition shows the originals as well as infra-red exposures, revealing x-ray images and blow-ups of details from the paintings.
