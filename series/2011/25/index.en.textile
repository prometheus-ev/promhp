An exhibition in Chemnitz is presenting graphic pieces and paintings by the impressionist Max Slevogt. Primarily the exhibits originate from the art collections Chemnitz and are complemented by some items on loan from the Saarlandmuseum Saarbücken. The selection comprises works of art  produced between 1887 and 1932, showing portraits of dancers, musicians and other public figures, landscapes, still life as well as designs for a Don Giovanni-staging of the Staatsoper Dresden and illustrations to „Lederstrumpf“ by James Cooper.
