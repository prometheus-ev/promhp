The „Nolde Stiftung Seebüll“ is providing an insight into different periods and topics of Noldes work of art. 
The exhibition is structured in four parts. One part is dealing with women-potraits, another one is presenting expressive fantasie watercolours from 1918. Gentle drawings reflecting the voyage to Japan in 1913/14 form a further domain and the great “Leben Christi” belongs to a group of biblical canvas.
