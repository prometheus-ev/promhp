In analytic series Imi Knoebel deals with questions concerning colour, material and space. An exhibition, compiled together with the artist, gives an insight into his work of art. On display will be pictures using collected materials originated in the early 1980th, pictures of the „Grace-Kelly“-series from the 1990th as well as works of the last decade, like the open rooms, composed of coloured aluminium panels.
