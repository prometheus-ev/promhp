A special exhibition in Halle is presenting art works of the artist group Brücke, dealing with landscape and nature. The oil and watercolour paintings as well as some drawings and prints originate from the collection Hermann Gerlinger and include some early pieces, which date back to the time before the foundation of the artist group in 1905.
Landscape as an image motif is display of the effort of the Brücke-artists to find originality and purity in art. Furthermore, Landscape is used to express subjective impressions and inner experiences.
