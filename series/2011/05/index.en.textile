A retrospective of Max Klingers artworks, alreday seen in Apolda, is now widend by the interesting confrontation with selected artworks by Wilhelm Lehmbruck. Klinger is accredited with an influence on Giorgio De Chirico, Käthe Kollwitz, Alfred Kubin, Max Bekmann and on the early work of Lehmbruck. The letter will be more understandable with the exhibition in Diusburg.

*"Max Klinger – Von der herben Zartheit schöner Formen":http://www.duisburg.de/micro2/lehmbruck/, 20. Januar – 24. April, Lehmbruck-Museum Duisburg*
