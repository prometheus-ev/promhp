A donation, containing five canvas and one drawing by Paula Modersohn-Becker as well as three canvas by Otto Modersohn, gives reason to an exhibition in the Albertinum in Dresden. The museum is presenting the new art works, among the still life showing here in the big picture, together with others hailing from private collections and from the museum inventory.
