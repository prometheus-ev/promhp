The photographer Thomas Struth (born in 1954) obtained international acceptance with numerous exhibitions in Europe and in the USA. Now the “Kunstsammlung Nordrhein Westfalen” is presenting the first retrospective of his work. Next to early works, like street-scenes and jungle-pictures („Paradises“) there are the famous museum-scenes („Audiences“) and a series of his new works:  Monumental photographs showing technical large-scale plants, like nuclear power stations, document and challenge a belief in progress, which is based only on technology and science.
