An exhibition in Hamburg is presenting images of human beings from different times and culture areas.  With more than 100 exhibits from the departments of Antiquity, China, Japan, Islam, European arts and crafts, graphic art, photography, fashion and furniture design they afford an overview of the collection.
The shown examples in this image series contain (with two exceptions) just European sculptures from the 18th to the 20th century.

*Body and Soul. Menschenbilder aus vier Jahrtausenden, bis 13.03.2011, Museum für Kunst und Gewerbe, Hamburg*
