After an exhibition in Wien was concentrated on portraits of the beginning Renaissance and the German language area, now an exhibition in Berlin will open, which deals with the Italien renaissance-portrait.

What does the portraits of young women, of merchants, of military commanders and churchmen 
reveal about the shown people? Inscriptions, clothing and jewellery as well as the background of the picture can say a lot about their status and (idealized) character traits.

The exhibition got loan collection from great museums and can present famous works of art, like the portrait of Simonetta Vespucci and that one of Giuliano de Medici by Botticelli, the Lady with an Ermine by Leonardo da Vinci or the so called „Bianca“ by Pollaiuolo.
