The variability of colours is characteristic for the video installations, the digital photomontages and the computer art, as well as the objects and the environments of Pipilotti Rist. The experimental film „Pepperminta“ uses colours not only as means of expression, but also as the central topic. Other important subjects in the work of the Swiss artist are feminism and the perception of the feminine body.
The Kunsthalle Bremen is presenting the audio-video-installation „Bremer Lungenflügel“ until the beginning of October.
