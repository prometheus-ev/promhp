Around 1500 the portrait was predominantly used as means of representation. Therefore  portraits of that time provide an interesting view on the society of the beginning Modern Era.
The Kunsthistorisches Museum in Vienna compiled over 140 excellent examples of his own collections as well as from important international collections and is presenting amongst others pictures by Albrecht Dürer, Lucas Cranach and Hans Holbein.

*"Dürer - Cranach – Holbein. The German Portrait around 1500":http://www.khm.at/en/kunsthistorisches-museum/exhibitions/current/duerer-cranach-holbein/, 31th May 2011 to 4th September 2011, Kunsthistorisches Museum, Vienna*
