Ralf Winkler, known as A. R. Penck, painted in 1984 a monumental canvas entitled “Me in Germany (west)”. Living in the DDR the artist got in trouble with the secret police and moved to West-Germany in 1980. The canvas deals with this new environment and uses symbols concerning basic subject areas like life, death, sexuality, clergy and power.
Because of the dimension the picture was exposed rarely and gives now reason to the exhibition in Cologne.

*A. R. Penck. Vergangenheit – Gegenwart – Zukunft, bis 20.03.2011, Museum Ludwig, Cologne*
