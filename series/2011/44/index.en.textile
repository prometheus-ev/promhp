The Galerie Neue Meister is presenting an exhibition concerning the New Objectivity, based on a research project. The aim of the project was to reconstruct a general view on the New Objectivity in Dresden in the 20s and to explicate the specific characteristics compared to other centres of realism of that time – like Hannover, Karlsruhe, Munich or Berlin.
Formed by the academy of arts and the arts and crafts school in Dresden a generation of artists, among Otto Dix, George Grosz, Otto Griebel, Hans Grundig, Wilhelm Lachnit und Bernhard Kretzschmar, painted realistic pictures, frequently added with irony and social criticism.
