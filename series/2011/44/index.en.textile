The Galerie Neue Meister is presenting an exhibition, based on a research project concerning the New Objectivity. The aim of the project was to reconstruct a general view on the New Objectivity in Dresden in the 20's and to explicate the specific characteristics compared to other centres of realism of that time, like Hannover, Karlsruhe, Munich or Berlin.
Formed by the academy of arts and the arts and crafts school in Dresden a generation of artists,  - among Otto Dix, George Grosz, Otto Griebel, Hans Grundig, Wilhelm Lachnit and Bernhard Kretzschmar - painted realistic pictures, frequently added with irony and social criticism.
