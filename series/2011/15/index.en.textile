Richard Long creates sculptures from collected natural materials - in the nature as well as in the museum. Simple forms like circle and rectangle play a dominant role.
Besides the „Berlin Circle“ from 1996 an exhibition in Berlin is presenting some other works arranged on the ground and the „River Avon Mud Circle“ in situ on the wall.
