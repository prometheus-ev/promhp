There are many photographs showing Picasso. They show him with works of art, with women, with  cigarette, with hat, in the costume of a sachem or of a torero, dancing in pants or laughing in the bathtub. Frequently he's looking direct into the camera. An exhibition in the Ludwig Museum raises the question of self-presentation. How strong are the creative influences of the photographers and of Picasso himself on the portrayal?
  

*"MemyselfandI. Photographic Portraits of Picasso":http://www.museum-ludwig.de/, 24.09.2011 – 15.01.2012, Museum Ludwig, Köln*
