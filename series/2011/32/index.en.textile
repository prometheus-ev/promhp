„I´m interested in the edge of the world, not the middle.“ 

Photographs by Sibylle Bergemann create a melancholic atmosphere, particular in the pictures of the current exhibition in the gallery C/O: the Polaroids. 
Original this kind of photos were not taken seriously, but today one sees the charm of Polaroids. They are limited to a square size, they are more exposed to transience and they are unique.
