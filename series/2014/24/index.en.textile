In the recent years the exact size replica of an art object, the copy, experiences increased interest in art historical research. Often, the focus is on factors, which by definition does not include the copy as the change of the original in a form of remixes, of innovative own performances. 
At the meeting in Hannover the original definition of a copy is in focus and discussed from different perspectives in order to develop new ideas for the future presentation and research.
