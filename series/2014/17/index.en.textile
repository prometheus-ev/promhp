At first sight viewers are captivated by his pictures. Then it goes on, because the charm in the work of the Flemish artist Pieter Bruegel the Elder (1525/30-1569) is always hidden in the details. Every little part of his complex works is intended by him and there are always new things to discover: a critical allusion to the recent discussions of his time or a humorous edge history. 
In the exhibition, the visitors can enjoy the artistic ingenuity of the complete printed oeuvre after designs by the Antwerp Master and can be transported to the world theatre of the 16th century.
