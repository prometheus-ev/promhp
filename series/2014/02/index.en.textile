What effect did the second world war have on the work of Alexej Jawlensky? That's one of the key-questions in an exhibition in Chemnitz. Moreover they are presenting paintings and graphic art from all periods of his work. The 150th anniversary of the painter's birthday is a good occasion to acknowledge the Jawlensky-collection of the Gunzenhauser foundation.
