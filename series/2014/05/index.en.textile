Barbara Klemm took this photo of Wolf Biermann during his legendary concert in Cologne in 1967, after which the DDR refused his re-entry. Her photographs show historical turning points, political events, but street scenes and portraits too. A retrospective at Martin-Gropius-Bau is presenting 300 exhibits and turns out the artistic qualities of Klemms journalistic photographies.
