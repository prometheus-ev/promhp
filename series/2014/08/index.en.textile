An exhibition at the Kunsthalle Emden is presenting woodcuts by Lyonel Feininger. While Feininger made just a few etchings and lithographies, the woodcuts represent a considerable part of his work. The exhibition in Emden also shows the importance of the woodcuts for Feininges paintings. Furthermore the painting „Schwarze Welle“, which is part of the Herni Nannen collection, delivers an occasion to focus on motifs with ships.
