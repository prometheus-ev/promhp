Inside the digital offering objects of studies, structured learning elements and curricular study units are differentiated.

Learning objects are the smallest units of meaning as image, text or animation.
Learning elements are structured compilations of study objects about one topic or one area of knowledge, e.g. topic rooms, a method-coach or a knowledge-coach.

A study unit is applied curricular so that it can accompany a present training sessions, be deployed in an online-workshop and/or be used for private studies.
