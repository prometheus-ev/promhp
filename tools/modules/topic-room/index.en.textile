h2. <%= page_title %>

The Topic room rests upon constructivist principles as it provides
* an authentic application context,
* multiple perspectives on the content,
* realistical and complex tasks of problem solving,
* adaptivity.

The adaptivity here becomes realised in
* arbitrary accesses and learning methods,
* graduated levels of information,
* graded facilities and
* different task challenges

and not on the basis of automatically created user profiles.

A subject-specific content, its objects, complex and realistic problem-situations and individual efforts concerning comprehensive intellectual questions are taking centre stage as shown in the following examples:
